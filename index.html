Featured Movies

The Avengers

Release Date:27 April 2012

Nick Fury of S.H.I.E.L.D. assembles a team of superheroes, including Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye, to stop Loki from invading Earth with an alien army. The film explores teamwork and the formation of the Avengers.

Avengers:Age of Ultron

Release Date:24 April 2015

The Avengers face a new threat when Tony Stark’s AI project, Ultron, becomes sentient and seeks to eradicate humanity. The team must unite against this powerful foe while also dealing with the emergence of new heroes, like Scarlet Witch and Vision.

Avengers Infinity War

Release Date: 27 April 2018

The Avengers and their allies must confront the Mad Titan, Thanos, who seeks to collect all six Infinity Stones to achieve his goal of wiping out half of all life in the universe. The film is an epic battle against time and overwhelming odds.

Avengers Endgame

Release Date: 26 April 2019

Following the devastating events of Infinity War, the surviving heroes devise a plan to reverse Thanos’ actions. They embark on a time heist to retrieve the Infinity Stones from the past, leading to an epic showdown that tests their resolve and sacrifices.

Avengers Doomsday

Release Date:27 May 2026

Doomsday is a formidable villain in the DC Comics universe, best known as the being who killed Superman in the "Death of Superman" storyline. Created through a combination of genetic engineering and evolution, he possesses immense strength, resilience, and the ability to adapt to and overcome any opponent. Doomsday's most notable trait is his capacity for regeneration, allowing him to come back stronger after each defeat.
